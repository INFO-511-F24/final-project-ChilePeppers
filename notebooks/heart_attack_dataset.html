dataset: heart_attack_dataset.csv

The dataset provides gender, age, bloood pressure, cholesterol, diabetes, smoking status, chest pain type, and treament summary for 1000 patients. The dataset has shape 1000 rows 8 columns. The dataset could be used to complete a statistical analysis of heart disease and complete hypothesis testing. The dataset could also be used to train a machine learning algoritm to predict treatment based on patient profile.
